Featured Performance

O je, Tannenbaum!

A dance piece for children aged 4 to 10

Christmas is just around the corner, it's cold outside and it smells of biscuits. All that's missing is the Christmas tree. The most beautiful tree in the forest is chosen and festively decorated at home. Candles glow in its branches and tinsel and ornaments glitter next to it. The Christmas tree is the star of the festive season. But as soon as the festive season is over, it is carelessly thrown out onto the street.

O je, Tannenbaum! is a Christmas dance piece that tells the journey of a Christmas tree in a playful and imaginative way: from cutting it down to decorating it and disposing of it. It invites children to think about sustainability: how can we show more respect for nature?

Flowing movement sequences, acrobatic duets and theatrical choreographies full of humour tell of the highs and lows of a friendship or a relationship between siblings. The dancers from the OOPSALA dance collective also encourage young audiences to join in.

Duration: 45 minutes

Workshop Option: A dance workshop for up to 25 people (30 or 50 minutes). No previous knowledge required, focus on fun and being together.
