Meet Our Team

The creative minds behind OOPSALA TanzKollektiv

Greta Schuster

Dancer, Choreographer & Teacher

Contemporary dance graduate from 'The Place' in London. Experienced dancer, choreographer and dance teacher who has worked with company 'Lunapark', Anima May and Lore Dekeysker. Her choreographies have been featured at Theater an der Parkaue, Brotfabrik and Alpsmove Festival.

David Mariano

Dancer, Teacher & Choreographer

Graduate from the National School of Classical and Contemporary Dance in Mexico City. A gifted teacher who helps people connect with their bodies through movement. Has worked professionally across Mexico, USA, Spain, Lithuania, Greece and Germany.

Eva Leemans

Dancer & Teacher

Graduate of Northern School of Contemporary Dance in Leeds and member of 'The Excessive human collective'. Active in Berlin's dance scene, performing in various productions and teaching both professional and amateur dancers.

Kerri Fitzsimons

Dancer, Choreographer & Art Mediator

Dance graduate from Bath Spa University, based in Berlin since 2019. Choreographer and dancer who presented 'under' at Lake Studios Berlin and DO Festival in Gdańsk. Also works as a Mediator at KW Institute for Contemporary Art.
