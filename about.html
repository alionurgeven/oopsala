Who is OOPSALA?

A Berlin-based collective of daring artists working in contemporary dance, education and theatre

Our Story

Founded in 2023, OOPSALA is a Berlin based collective of daring artists working in the fields of contemporary dance, education and theatre. Our aim is to introduce children to the world of dance and contemporary art through fun projects that prioritise play and curiosity. Our workshop style performances encourage participation, stimulate the imagination and at the same time explore ecological and social issues.

Why We Do This

Emotional Connection

We believe in discussing ecological and social issues through aesthetic, scenic and visual language that appeals to emotions and creates empathy.

Accessible Communication

We tell stories through behavior, gesture, touch and movement, finding accessible ways to communicate abstract topics to children.

Sustainable Practice

We prioritize environmental protection through adaptable productions, thrifted costumes, and minimal, versatile props.
